# Character List

|Character|Description|
|---------|-----------|
|Odysseus|The protagonist of _The Odyssey_. Odysseus fought among the other Greek heros at Troy and now struggles to return to his kingdom in Ithaca. Odysseus is the husband of Queen Penelope and the father of Prince Telemachus. Though a strong and courageous warrior. he is most renowned for his cunning. He is a favorite of the goddess Athena, who often sends him divine aid, but a bitter enemy of Poseidon, who frustates his journey at every turn.|
